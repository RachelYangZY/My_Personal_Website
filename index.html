About Me



Hi! I'm Rachel Yang, a third year Engineering Science student at the University of Toronto. I'll be majoring in Electrical and Computer Engineering and minoring in Bioengineering and Artificial Intelligence. My interests lie in a wide variety of fields:

  • Hardware programming: I love tinkering with microcontrollers and building gadgets!
  • Front-end design: I often say that a big contribution to my love of front-end design stemmed from the school newspaper that I was Editor-in-Chief for. The organization and user engagement strategies formed a foundation for crafting beatiful, user-friendly interfaces.
  • Artificial Intelligence: Whether it be utilizing APIs or building Neural Networks from the fundamentals, I know my way around utilizing these models to create something useful.
  • Biology: My favourite subject will forever be biology, and I have competed in medical competitions, delved into independent research projects, and extensively studied the intricacies of life.
  • Figure skating: My absolute favourite relaxation activity is gliding and spinning across the ice.

Despite the breadth of my interests, each one receives my full dedication and passion. Learn more about how I've pursued knowledge in my "Past Experiences" and "Personal Projects" section!
